About Us

Angela's Daycare is a non-profit organisation aimed at taking care of the children of parents who are busy or those less fortunate. We provide the children with meals at school and food to take home.

We believe the foundational phase of learning is important and all children have the right to the same education. Founded by Angela Sithole in 2000, we've been serving the Newlands community for over 25 years with love, care, and quality early education.
